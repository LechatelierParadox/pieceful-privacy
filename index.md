# Privacy Policy for Pieceful Play

**Last Updated: January 31, 2026**

## Information We Do NOT Collect

Pieceful Play is designed with privacy as a priority. We DO NOT:
- Collect any personal information
- - Track user behavior
  - - Store photos on our servers
    - - Share data with third parties
      - - Transmit any data over the internet
       
        - ## Information Stored Locally on Your Device
       
        - All data is stored ONLY on your device:
        - - Photos you select for puzzles
          - - Puzzle progress
            - - Completed puzzles gallery
              - - App settings
               
                - This data never leaves your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.
               
                - ## Photo Library Access
               
                - We request photo access ONLY to let you select photos for puzzles. We:
                - - Only access photos you explicitly choose
                  - - Never scan your entire photo library
                    - - Never upload photos anywhere
                      - - Never share photos with anyone
                       
                        - ## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)
                       
                        - Pieceful Play is for children ages 2-8. We comply with COPPA. We do NOT:
                        - - Collect personal information from children
                          - - Enable communication features
                            - - Link to social media
