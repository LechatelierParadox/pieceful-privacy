# pieceful-privacy
Privacy Policy for Pieceful Play
Last Updated: February 6, 2026

1. Introduction
Welcome to Pieceful Play! This privacy policy explains how we handle your information. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it, especially for our younger users.

2. Information Collection
We do not collect any personal information.

We do not ask for your name, email, or phone number.
We do not track your location.
We do not collect data about how you use the app.
3. Photo Access
Pieceful Play requires access to your device's Camera and Photo Library to allow you to create puzzles from your own pictures.

All photos remain on your device.
We never upload, share, or transmit your photos to any server or third party.
Photos are processed locally on your phone or tablet.
4. Third-Party Services
We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking services. The app is a self-contained experience.

5. Children’s Privacy
Our app is designed for children and families. We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.
6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: lechatelier.paradox@proton.me
